Introduction
Shoptrue is a fashion discovery company.It develops powerful insights and capabilities to help match people to relevant brands and styles. It provides millions of shoppers a unique one-stop personal shop so that they can find the right expressions of them.
The Mission

The Opportunity
  • Hassle-free online shopping experience
  • No worries about ill fitting products or hours of browsing
  • Personalized product suggestions curated with AI technology
  • Enhancing the overall shopping experience
The Challenge - What?
  • Browsing through and selecting from thousands of products can be laborious and time consuming
  • Finding the perfect fit while shopping for clothes online can be quite challenging

PERSONALIZATION

Context
Shoptrue's primary emphasis was on providing tailored product recommendations to its users. During our brainstorming sessions, we explored innovative ways to bring this concept to fruition, ultimately conceiving the Style Quiz as a solution.

Style Quiz was the point where we can get to know our users and their preferences & needs.
Challenges
These were the challenges which were in our mind while building the style quiz -
  • Why user will be motivated to do minimum 74 clicks for the style quiz.
  • Good navigation and flow of the quiz so user will complete the quiz and not quit.
  • Quiz has to be fun,simple and interactive.
Finalized Style Quiz questions
In order to maintain a high level of user engagement and excitement, we made the strategic decision to create a concise and enjoyable style quiz that would require minimal clicks to complete.

the questions which we finalized included:
  • What are you shopping for?
  • Tell us how you dress in a typical month?
  • How would you define your style personality?
  • Now, select a few brands you usually wear.
  • What colors do you wear (or what to wear more of)?
  • What patterns do you like to wear?
  • How much you usually spend on these items?
  • Please provide your email
