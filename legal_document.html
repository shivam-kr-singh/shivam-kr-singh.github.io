How can a simple question cause so much trouble?

Project Details
Mobile App Design To Understand The Legal Document Before Signing It
Timeline : Sep 26 - Sep 30, 2022 (5 Days)
My Role : Personal Project
Problem
Fail to understand the different aspect of legal documents before signing it

As a user without the knowledge of law or little knowledge, It’s very difficult to understand the different aspect of legal documents. After spending plenty of time user got frustate and take the document to a legal consultant.

Solution

  1. Comparison against a standard template
  • Shows the risk level of the document
  • Categorize the document information under the important aspect
  • Detect the important and technical term and explain it in simple way
  1. Second Opinion
  • Consult a professional or share your report to community / friends to ask their opinion
  • Instant schedule call to a professional depending on your ability
  • Help to understand the document more deeper

How did we do it?

Research
Starting with the desktop research, I have gone through the activities that people doing before signing a legal document. After analyzing the whole activities categorize in 3 scenarios.
  1. User don’t have time
  1. User have time and want to understand the document in deeper
  1. Document is not so important / Familiar with document / Trust on sender
Major Insight
Why people sign a legal document without a clear understanding

After analyzing the above activities done by the people, we got some points due to which people sign the document without understand it.

  • Lack of time
  • Too technical to understand
  • Get frustrate to read the long document
  • What is important to look in document
  • Not an important document
  • I am familiar to it
  • I can trust on sender
Pain Points
  • Time consuming
  • Too technical and long that cause frustation and hard to understand
  • Difficult to find the important aspect to look in documents
Important aspect in a legal document

There are different aspect that should be consider before signing a legal document and after analyzing, I have divided it in 4 categorize:

  1. About Parties
  • Identification of parties
  • Right of parties
  • Duties of parties
  1. About Payment
  • Cost of offer
  • Payment terms
  1. Law and Clauses
  • Choice of law clauses
  • Disclaimers
  • Dispute resolution clauses
  • Severability
  1. Warranties
  • Time period
  • Validities
Possible solutions

After doing the desktop research, I came across three different directions that can be useful to solve the pain-point to some extent:

  1. Modification of document
  • Minimize the use of technical word
  • Provide definition and explanation
  • Provide visual aids
  1. Ask to someone
  • Legal consultant
  • Friends and relatives
  • A community where we can ask the questions
  1. Technology based solution
  • Compare the document with standard document
  • Use of AI which can detect and explain the important terms

Upon thinking the above solution, It concluded that Modification of document is on the government hand and To ask someone is a time taking and a lengthy process but it can be as a second opinion and the of technology is the fastest way. So I stuck on Technology based solution.

Concept generation

A platform with the below features can be created to solve the pain-points:

  • Compare the document with standard document gives an understanding of the document
  • Prioritize the different aspect of a legal document
  • Detect the important and technical term and explain it in simple way
  • Analyze the risk of a legal document
  • Consult to someone can be a second opinion: Community feed and hire a consultant can be a feature of this platform
